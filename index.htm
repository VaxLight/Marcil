About Us

We are a vibrant and dynamic mega church that brings together people from all walks of life to worship, learn, and grow together in faith. Join us as we build a community of love, service, and purpose. We are committed to spreading the Word of God. Our church is a place where people can find solace, inspiration, and a place to grow spiritually.

Projects

Current Projects

We are currently running several projects, some of which include:

  • Marcil TV: A community media project aimed at reaching the majority of people in the community, with an estimated reach of 80% of the community. This project will bring news, entertainment, and community updates to the people.
  • Excellent Youth Center: An ongoing construction project aimed at providing a dedicated space for young people in the community. This center will serve as a hub for various activities and support, and is currently under construction.
  • Marcil Pure Water Project: A new initiative focused on providing clean and accessible water to communities living in remote areas.
